Announcing Winter Break Classes

Since 2018, 5 students and graduates from the Harvard Graduate School of Education (link to external website) have worked together to provide high quality real-time learning programs to students online. We are a charitably-centered organization, donating 100% of profits to charity (to date we have donated $25,000). Past recipients include: The International Rescue Committee (Refugee Education), the National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI), Girl Up and Dandelion Middle School.

January 8th-20th, Students for Students will be offering our third annual session winter classes. Specifically in the winter of 2020, we will be leading the following small scale learning groups:

  • International Inquiry Groups (reading a text based in a country outside of the US, with emphasis on writing, discussion and research)
  • Exploring Computer Science with Python
